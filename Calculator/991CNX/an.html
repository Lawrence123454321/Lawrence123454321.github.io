Screen Glitches

Here are the procedure for a general screen glitch.

  1. Open your calculator.
  2. MENU 2 SHIFT MENU 1 3
  3. 8 SHIFT 8 ↓ 2 7 = ↑ ←
  4. SHIFT . ← ← → ENG (i) ← 9 DEL DEL
  5. Type 100 characters and a plus sign
  6. Determine the numbers before an: for example, 110 an is 110 characters before the 'an'
  7. Type [Number before an - 101] characters (ex. 9 characters if 110 an)
  8. =
